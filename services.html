Comment Toxicity Classification | Python, Numpy, Pandas, Tensorflow


Implemented a 𝗕𝗶𝗱𝗶𝗿𝗲𝗰𝘁𝗶𝗼𝗻𝗮𝗹 𝗟𝗦𝗧𝗠 𝗺𝗼𝗱𝗲𝗹 (𝗥𝗡𝗡) 𝘂𝘀𝗶𝗻𝗴 𝗧𝗲𝗻𝘀𝗼𝗿𝗙𝗹𝗼𝘄 to classify text comments into multiple toxicity categories, including toxic, severe toxic, obscene, threat, insult, and identity hate.
